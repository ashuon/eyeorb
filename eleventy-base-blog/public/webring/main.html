what is a webring?

from wikipedia: A webring (or web ring) is a collection of websites linked together in a circular structure, usually organized around a specific theme, and often educational or social. They were popular in the 1990s and early 2000s, particularly among amateur websites.

what are the rules?

the cosplayer webring is for all people who cosplay and are interested in finding other cosplayers on the indie web. however, you may not join if:

  • you harass or 'call out' others for reasons that are not inherently harmful to another human being - whether it be over appearance, their taste in media/fiction (even if not platable to you), their hobbies, their cosplay skill level, etc
  • you are genuinely bigoted or generally hateful, or host hateful content
  • you have a dni list of any sort on the indie web
  • you don't have a personal website, coded by you - this webring does NOT include carrd, rentry, strawpage, etc.
